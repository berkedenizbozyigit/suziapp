How she works

You tell her. She finds it.
You swipe it. She sorts it.

Four steps. No more lost tabs.

01

You tell her

Chat with Suzi. Tell her what you need — your vibe, your budget, your size.

"A dress for my friend's wedding. Seaside, glamorous, nothing too bright."
02

She finds

She digs the internet. Brings back the gems. Compares prices, picks the best deals.

"Same dress at three shops — grab the one at ASOS, £30 cheaper."
03

You swipe

One swipe at a time. Save what you love, skip what you don't. She learns as you go.

"Liked this one — saved to your Wedding picks."
04

She sorts

Folders organise themselves. She tracks drops, restocks, price changes — and pings you.

"That blazer dropped 30% — grab it before it's gone."

Why Suzi exists

Online shopping is chaos.

You open one tab. Then another. Then ten more. You lose the good one. Your focus drifts. Suddenly there are 100 tabs and your head is spinning.

Too many shops. Too many options. Too many filters. No one to tell you "this one's nice, this isn't."

Suzi turns the chaos into a game. She brings calm and order to your shopping — like the friend who actually knows fashion, knows your taste, and has the time to look.

I came up with Suzi after getting lost between endless tabs while looking for something specific to wear.

What started as excitement turned into frustration — too many websites, too many options, no easy way to actually discover the right piece.

So I thought: there has to be a smarter way to shop online.

That's how Suzi was born.
